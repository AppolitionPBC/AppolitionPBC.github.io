Announcing The Appolition Partner/Grantee Program

Appolition is opening our doors and expanding our granting system to include dozens of bail funds across the United States. From Boston to New York, down to Atlanta, through New Orleans, up to Chicago and all the way to Seattle, Portland and our hometown of Oakland.

We’re excited to work with activists and organizations across the country in the name of tackling a mighty, predatory cash bail system.
