Is my fund or organization eligible to receive Appolition funds?
Appolition raises funds and makes monthly payouts directly to organizations having both a mission doing direct service work toward the goal ending mass incarceration practices & policies.
Appolition currently prioritizes funding for organizations who operate community bail funds.
We prefer that our Grantee Partners be either a 501c3 or fiscally sponsored however we are open to working with organizations of other structures.
How do I apply?
Complete the form at: Appolition.fund. Email hello+partner@appolition.us with any questions.
When will my org’s application be reviewed?
Applications are reviewed monthly. Within 30 days of submission, you will be notified of eligibility and receive part 2 of the Grantee Partner application form.
When will I start receiving Appolition funds?
We on-board between 1 - 2 organizations per month depending on user growth.
How much will my fund or org receive in donations?
We do not guarantee any specific amount as monthly payouts vary by user activity however we’ve made payouts between $2,000 & $20,000 in previous months of operation.
When will payouts occur?
Payouts occurs monthly -- for the previous 30 days of donations. For example, donations collected during the month of February are paid out on March 31st.
Do you have to be a 501c3 to be an Appolition Grantee Partner?
We prefer that our Grantee Partners be either a 501c3 or fiscally sponsored however we are open to working with organizations of other structures.
